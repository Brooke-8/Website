ABOUT

Hi, I'm Brooke!

I am an undergraduate student pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computer Engineering at the University of Ottawa. I have a huge interest in software and computer design and love creating things that I can use in my day-to-day life. It is important to me that all my projects are accessible so that others can build on and enjoy my work. To this end, I strive to write detailed documentation alongside each of my projects, but if you run into any difficulties, feel free to reach out to me using the contact links to the left.

Outside of programming, I currently work as a lifeguard and swim instructor. Swimming and aquatics are very close to my heart, as I was a competitive swimmer for 10 years. I also dabble in various forms of digital art, including painting and 3D modeling. One of my favorite mediums to work in is pixel art as it allows me to focus on color and composition!

On this website, you can find a collection of all my projects, as well as some hobbies I'm proud of, such as my pixel art <3
